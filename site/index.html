Tonediff

How to play

After clicking the start button, you will hear two tones of different frequency. The point of this page is to see if you can tell which one of them is higher pitched! When the tones stop:

  • Press the ↑ key if the second tone is higher pitched.
  • Press the ↓ key if the second tone is lower pitched.
  • Press the Enter key to listen to the two tones again.
